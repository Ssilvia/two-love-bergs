Packing

With the caribbean climate in Aruba you should wear comfortable clothes that you can wear in the heat. Breathable and lighter clothing is optimal to keep you from overheating. Pack shoes you're comfortable dancing in the sand with and another pair you're comfortable lounging on the beach with. For the wedding pack formal/cocktail-party outfits that won't be too hot to wear throughout the day.

The weather will be H-O-T! Aruba will be very hot in June (it's warm all year round). Expect lots of sunshine.
